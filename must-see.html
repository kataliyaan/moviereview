Must-See Movies Chosen by Authors

Welcome to our collective guide to must-see movies, brought to you by the authors. Each of us has selected a range of films that have not only entertained us but also left a lasting impact on us. Our choices span various genres, styles, and eras, showcasing the diversity and richness of cinema. Whether you're a film aficionado or simply looking for your next great movie night, these selections promise to captivate, inspire, and provoke thought. Dive into our curated list and discover why these films are essential viewing for anyone who loves a good story.

1

Past Lives

Rating: PASSED
Genre: Romance/Drama
Release Date: June 02, 2023
Director: Celine Song
Cast: Greta Lee, Teo Yoo, and John Magaro.
Runtime: 106 Minutes
★★★★★

Nora and Hae Sung, two deeply connected childhood friends, are wrest apart after Nora's family emigrates from South Korea. Decades later, they are reunited for one fateful week as they confront destiny, love and the choices that make a life.

2

Hello, Love, Goodbye

Rating: PASSED
Genre: Romance/Drama
Release Date: July 31, 2019
Director: Cathy Garcia-Sampana
Cast: Kathryn Bernardo and Alden Richards
Runtime: 118 Minutes
★★★★★

A bartender and a domestic helper of Filipino origin living in Hong Kong find themselves falling in love, but they each have different plans for their future.

3

A Walk to Remember

Rating: PASSED
Genre: Romance/Drama
Release Date: January 25, 2002
Director: Adam Shankman
Cast: Shane West, Mandy Moore, Peter Coyote and Daryl Hannah
Runtime: 102 Minutes
★★★★

Set in North Carolina, "A Walk To Remember" follows the rite of passage of a jaded, aimless high school senior (Shane West) who falls in love with a guileless young woman (Mandy Moore) he and his friends once scorned. The two develop a powerful and inspirational relationship in which they discover truths that take most people a lifetime to learn.

4

Lost and Delirious

Rating: PASSED
Genre: Romance/Drama
Release Date: January 21, 2001
Director: Léa Pool
Cast:
Runtime: 104 Minutes
★★★★
Piper Perabo, Jessica Paré, Mischa Barton, Jackie Burroughs, Mimi Kuzyk

"Lost and Delirious" is about the friendship of three teenagers and how they experience it in a private school. Throughout the film, the lost girls question their relationships with one another and the authority of others while desperately attempting to seek out true love.
